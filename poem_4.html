Poem 4

A Note to Self

Home | All Poems

Clearly you were the rotten potato
But it's sad because it affects the whole bag
You were even happy to see me low
Your brain was full of air like an airbag.

You thought you pulled me down
But in reality, you were the clown
You hate me with all your heart for not giving you my card
Damn girl your life must have been hard.

You threw dirt on me, trying to kill me
But you didn't know you grew a wildflower
And actually, setting me free
Felt very nice like water running in the body after a long day - Shower.

You thought you were the prize
But you were just a salty spice
Now that you are no longer here life feels nice
Like a soft drink on a hot day with ice.

Since you left my life
I feel like something has been pulled out of my back - a knife
I really survived a wildlife
Turned a shelf-life to real-life.

This is a piece of advice to myself and everyone reading this poem
Know your worth and don't stay where you will end up broken
Love may hurt but I was never meant to kill
Love is not perfect but it conquers all when all are in love
Not one way
When they don't love you, leave because you deserve more.
