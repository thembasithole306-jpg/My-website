Poem 4

A Note to Self

Home | All Poems

Hey!

This poem won't be going to you
I guess it's like a little note to self
I guess it's time I become true,
Take the books out of the shelf.

It's been long now, they are even dusty
I guess I was lost in the act of "Love"
Even metal decays over time and gets rusty
Nothing stays forever like the one above.

But I wished our bond could be forever
Because being with you was always better
Than this feeling of being apart, however
No one knows the future - no man is that clever.

We shared love so true
Everyday all I thought about was you
Time we spent together - Quality
I never knew one of us lacked honesty.

You stayed with me, because of what I offered
Not because of the love
In a nick of time you left like water
Getting hit by the sun and rising like a dove (Evaporation)

You tried to pin all this on me
When they asked, you said "ask him"
Because you were chasing the green, all about the tree
I never thought I was in the dim.

Your friends believed I was the one to blame
They were all under your control like in a game
Now they are all at hate
But I don't care so their hate doesn't have weight.

You turned all of them against
Well that's what you thought
I am an overthinker, so all this was sensed
We reached a point of a tie - a knot.

I had to cut all ties
Because I told you countless times that I hate lies
By all ties I meant everything that connects with you
So that I stay with only what's true.

Clearly you were the rotten potato
But it's sad because it affects the whole bag
You were even happy to see me low
Your brain was full of air like an airbag.

You thought you pulled me down
But in reality, you were the clown
You hate me with all your heart for not giving you my card
Damn girl your life must have been hard.

You threw dirt on me, trying to kill me
But you didn't know you grew a wildflower
And actually, setting me free
Felt very nice like water running in the body after a long day - Shower.

You thought you were the prize
But you were just a salty spice
Now that you are no longer here life feels nice
Like a soft drink on a hot day with ice.

Since you left my life
I feel like something has been pulled out of my back - a knife
I really survived a wildlife
Turned a shelf-life to real-life.

This is a piece of advice to myself and everyone reading this poem
Know your worth and don't stay where you will end up broken
Love may hurt but I was never meant to kill
Love is not perfect but it conquers all when all are in love
Not one way
When they don't love you, leave because you deserve more.
