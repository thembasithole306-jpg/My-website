Poem 3

The Day Your Eyes Forgot Me

Home | All Poems

Hey!

I don't even know if you will respond to this,
Or to even try to read it at least,
Because lately we have different wholes, iris,
I could swear one of us turned to a beast.

A beast that attacks but it's blind,
Beast so strong, all it knows is to destroy,
Crushing everything like it was never once kind,
Like it was never loved and like it didn't enjoy.

Said to be strong, never cries,
But what not crying does to one onside, dies,
One thing that can send someone deep to boil like fries,
That will definitely be lies.

I remember how we were happy together,
under sunny day favored even by the weather,
never felt so light like a feather,
Planned the future together.

And that made me happy,
Never even thought everything was snappy,
Caught all the body hair raise,
Didn't understand what was going on when I gazed.

My friends told me it meant something,
And I was stupid enough to believe them,
Till you stopped one thing that had us together, Loving,
You used me and left me pressed and salted like a ham.

I asked myself, what did I do?
For you to change all of a sudden, to treat me so bad,
Because you changed out of the blue,
You never saw me cry, because we are taught that a man never shows when sad.

Then I got a word out and investigated,
Found out that you cannot use a spare wheel, if you don't have a puncture (Flat tire),
But all this was predicted,
I applauded myself I was like a hunter.

So now I knew what it feels like to be loved by words and not actions,
So you found yourself another guy,
I was just one of the distractions,
You said I was your air, I guess he is the whole sky.

You thought your secret was safe,
All along you thought you were in a cave,
Until I said "don't think I was just born,
I know about you and John."

You were scared that I might hurt you,
But I had a different view,
Cos when you cheated you had one too,
So I'll rather not disturb you two.

No wonder we didn't see eye to eye,
When you saw me, you couldn't even say hi,
I guess you have an eye that didn't see me anymore,
That's why I decided to point to you the door.

I never wanted to settle the score,
I chose myself over war,
I know my worth,
Known that since birth.

Mother raised a gentleman,
That knows how to deal with fire like a fireman,
I don't know what you saw in the other guy,
But I can say it is your loss, not mine.
