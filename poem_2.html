Ecoes of the soul

Hey!

Yeah, I know, I know

It’s me again
Don’t worry, I am not writing in pain
Ever since I met you, I don’t know how that feels
There’s this constant motion inside me, like spinning wheels

What I mean is that it never stops
It keeps turning, climbing, rising, it hops
It makes time want to pause, reality bends
Each heartbeat a question, every glance transcends

No feeling has been greater than this
Kept on going up like an elevator’s bliss
Got my head spun around like an operator
Mind and heart agreed: all we see is danger
Yet it poked my chest, a sly love instigator
Everything feels exact, like answers on a calculator

I still don’t understand how her presence is so strong
My eyes, my mind, my heart can’t measure for long
Is she a rainbow? Because she’s vivid and bright
A treasure, a reminder of life’s pure light
All I could do was let my eyes capture the view
And my chest fluttered with every shade, every hue

I don’t even want to start talking about her face
The kind that needs no filter, the perfect grace
Best in the cage — Ace, pristine and serene
Never seen a face so effortlessly clean

Ok, let me cut the chase
Be like a full stop — get straight to the place
But my heart’s still running a marathon, a race
Every thought of her feels like a checkpoint to embrace

And yet, these wheels keep spinning in my chest
The thrill, the awe, the feeling I can’t rest
Each glance, each smile, a pulse that won’t fade
A constant, sweet chaos, a melody she made
