Poem 2

Echoes of the Soul

In the quiet corners of my mind,
Your whispers linger, soft yet defined.
Memories rise like the tide of the sea,
Pulling me deeper into what used to be.

I chase shadows of laughter long past,
Moments I thought forever would last.
But time moves swift, and hearts must part,
Leaving fragments scattered across the heart.

Yet even in the ache, I find a spark,
A light that shines when the world is dark.
Through echoes of the soul, I hear your song,
Reminding me of where I belong.
